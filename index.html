Explore

Dive into the wonders of our world, from breathtaking landscapes to hidden natural marvels. Every corner holds a story, every journey sparks curiosity, and every view takes your breath away.
Adventure awaits, come explore the mysteries of nature like never before.

Salar de Uyuni (Salt Flats), Bolivia

The world’s largest salt flat turns into a perfect mirror after rain a surreal reflection of the sky that blurs the line between heaven and earth.

Mount Roraima (Venezuela/Brazil/Guyana)

A flat-topped “table mountain” often shrouded in mist, believed by Indigenous people to be the home of gods and spirits.

Antelope Canyon USA

Carved by flash floods, its flowing red sandstone walls glow with shifting light — like walking through a dream of fire.

Ice Caves of Vatnajökull, Iceland

Inside Iceland’s largest glacier lie glowing blue caves that melt and reform each year nature's living architecture.

Zhangjiajie National Forest, China

Towering stone pillars inspired the floating mountains in Avatar. Fog drifts among them like a silent sea.

Grand Prismatic, Spring USA

The largest hot spring in the U.S., with rainbow-colored rings formed by heat-loving microbes — a natural kaleidoscope.

Bora Bora Lagoon, French Polynesia

Its waters shift between turquoise and sapphire, encircled by volcanic peaks — a paradise seemingly painted by gods.

Plitvice Lakes, Croatia

Sixteen lakes connected by waterfalls in emerald forests — they change color depending on light, minerals, and moss.

Cappadocia, Turkey

Volcanic “fairy chimneys” and ancient cave dwellings at sunrise, hundreds of hot air balloons drift above like souls in the wind

Waitomo Glowworm Caves, New Zealand

A galaxy of glowworms illuminates the cave ceilings like sailing under a subterranean night sky.

Who I Am & Why I Built Nature Calls

My name is Abraham, and I created Nature Calls for one simple reason: nature inspires me. The world is filled with forests, waterfalls, and mysterious landscapes that most people never get to see and I wanted to change that.

Nature Calls is my space to explore, document, and share the beauty of these hidden places. Every picture, every story, and every discovery on this website is a piece of my curiosity and passion for the natural world.

My mission is simple: help people feel the magic of nature again.
